ℹ️ About Pediatric Clinical Hub Framework

The Pediatric Clinical Hub is an advanced, client-side, zero-latency bedside assistance platform compiled to deliver decision tracking in high-throughput Outpatient Departments (OPD), Wards, and Neonatal Intensive Care units.

🎯 Clinical Objective

Eliminating transcription errors, manual arithmetic calculation delays, and lookup friction during high-stress encounters by aggregating clinical trackers into an unbreakable, device-private sandbox model ecosystem.

👥 Targets & Impact Scope

Consultants: Rapid nutritional gaps mapping & letterhead Rx printing.
Postgraduates (MD): Native GIR math, APGAR profiling, and Code Blue emergency loops tracker values.
MBBS Undergraduates: Direct access to standard Weech formulas context blocks.
