About Pediatric Clinical Hub

The Pediatric Clinical Hub is an advanced, client-side, zero-latency bedside assistance platform compiled to deliver decision tracking in high-throughput OPDs and NICUs.

All computations execute entirely client-side preserving strict data privacy parameters. No patient data leaves your device.
