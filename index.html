Quick Counseling & Household Measures

Household Portions

  • 1 bowl cooked rice (100g): 120 kcal, 2g Protein
  • 1 Idli / 1 Vada: 50 kcal
  • 1 Dosa / 1 Chapati: 70 kcal, 2g Protein
  • Cow's Milk (100ml): 65 kcal, 3.2g Protein
  • Breast Milk (100ml): 65 kcal, 1.1g Protein
  • 1 Egg (60g): 60 kcal, 6g Protein
  • Mashed Potato (1 no): 40 kcal

  • Vitamin C: Amla, Citrus fruits, Guava, Capsicum
  • Calcium: Milk, Ragi, Greens, Soya bean, Dry coconut
  • Iron: Jaggery, Dates, Watermelon, Greens, Meat, Raisins

Important Anthropometric Formulas (Weech's):

• Expected Weight (3-12 Months): (Age in months + 9) ÷ 2 kg
• Expected Weight (1-6 Years): (Age in years × 2) + 8 kg
• Expected Weight (7-12 Years): [(Age in years × 7) - 5] ÷ 2 kg
• Expected Height (2-12 Years): (Age in years × 6) + 77 cm
• Ponderal Index (PI): [Weight in g × 100] ÷ (Height in cm)3
